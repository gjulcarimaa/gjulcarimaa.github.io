Gerson Julcarima A.

Bio

I’m a PhD student and research assistant in the Department of Political Science at the University of Alberta. I received a bachelor’s degree in Social Sciences with a major in Sociology from the Universidad Nacional Mayor de San Marcos (UNMSM) and a M.A. in Political Science from the University of Lethbridge (Alberta, Canada).

Furthermore, I’m currently an affiliated researcher at the Instituto de Estudios Políticos Andinos (IEPA) and also coordinator of the Certificate Program in Religion and Politics at the Faculty of Social Sciences at UNMSM.
