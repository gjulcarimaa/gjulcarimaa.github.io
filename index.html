Gerson Julcarima A.

I am a graduate student in the Department of Political Science at the University of Lethbridge, Alberta (Canada).

My dissertation focuses on the institutionalization (or deinstitutionalization) processes of party systems in developing countries with an emphasis on Asia and Latin America. Additionally, my other research themes and topics of interest are the electoral violence or conflict as well as evangelicals and politics in Latin America.

I completed my undergraduate studies in sociology at the Universidad Nacional Mayor de San Marcos in Lima (Peru), and then I also pursued postgraduate studies at the Catholic University of Peru and the Methodist University of San Pablo (Brazil). From 2013 to 2018, I was an assistant professor at the University of Lima .

Currently, I am also a research associate of the Instituto de Estudios Políticos Andinos .
