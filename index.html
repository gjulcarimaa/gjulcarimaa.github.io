Gerson Julcarima A.

I am a graduate student in the Department of Political Science at University of Lethbridge, Alberta, Canada.

My dissertation focuses on the institutionalization (or deinstitutionalization) processes of party systems in developing countries with an emphasis in the Latin American and the Sub Saharan African regions. Additionally, my other research and of interest topics are the electoral violence or conflict as well as evangelicals and politics in Latin America.

I completed my undergraduate studies in sociology at the Universidad Nacional Mayor de San Marcos in Lima (Peru), and then I also pursued postgraduates studies at the Catholic University of Peru and the Methodist University of San Pablo (Brazil). During 2013-2018, I was an assistant professor at the University of Lima .

Currently, I am also a research associate of the Instituto de Estudios Políticos Andinos .
